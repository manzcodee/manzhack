Understanding Phishing Links

The Threat

Attackers often use URL shorteners or look-alike domains (e.g., sh0pee.com) to trick users into believing they are on a legitimate site.

The Defense

Always verify the domain name in the address bar. If a link redirects you unexpectedly, close the tab immediately. Never grant permissions to unknown sites.
